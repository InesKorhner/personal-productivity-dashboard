# Color System - Design Tokens

## Standardizovani Color Sistem

### Primary Colors
- **Primary**: `bg-primary text-primary-foreground` - za glavne akcije
- **Secondary**: `bg-secondary text-secondary-foreground` - za sekundarne akcije
- **Accent**: `bg-accent text-accent-foreground` - za hover states

### Semantic Colors
- **Destructive**: `bg-destructive text-destructive-foreground` - za delete/error akcije
- **Muted**: `bg-muted text-muted-foreground` - za disabled/secondary tekst
- **Card**: `bg-card text-card-foreground` - za kartice

### Status Colors (koristiti design tokens)
- **Success**: `bg-success text-success-foreground` - za success poruke
- **Warning**: `bg-warning text-warning-foreground` - za warning poruke
- **Info**: `bg-info text-info-foreground` - za info poruke

### Border Colors
- **Default**: `border-border` - za standardne border-e
- **Input**: `border-input` - za input polja

### Text Colors
- **Foreground**: `text-foreground` - za glavni tekst
- **Muted**: `text-muted-foreground` - za sekundarni tekst
- **Primary**: `text-primary` - za primary akcije
- **Destructive**: `text-destructive` - za error tekst
